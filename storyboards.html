storyboard 1

in this storyboard i focus on a UC davis student who is feeling lost in finding their place in their community. they want to have an interaction with others but they are averted to big groups or joining clubs. this site provides them with a way to both identify themselves through self-expression and to visualize the diversity of their community. my feedback for this storyboard was that the input is a bit vague, it is not clear what or how the user inputs, and the result was also confusing to them.

storyboard 2

therefore, with the second storyboard, i listened to the feedback by making the method of input more clear as a drop-down menu rather than a text input, and that they are engaging directly with their community. the choices that they make correspond to a visual input on the planet forms. i wanted it to be more clear that their motivation is the activity of other fellow students and the end result is visualizing the beauty of the diversity of their community and their unique place in it.
